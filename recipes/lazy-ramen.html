Lazy Ramen!

History and Description

Ramen something something else something something Something additional; paragraph 2

Ingredients

  • 1 serving of Ramen Noodles
  • Vegan Chicken Bullion
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 tsp MSG
  • 1 tsp Agar Agar Powder
  • Green Onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil. Stir in "Chicken" Bullion. After "Chicken" Bullion dissolved, add agar agar and MSG. Allow to cool some.
  2. Boil Ramen noodles in separate water, cook until al dente.
  3. In serving bowl, add cooked noodles then broth. Top with green onions.
