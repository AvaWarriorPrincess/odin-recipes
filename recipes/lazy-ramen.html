Description

This recipe is for a nice low calorie vegan ramen noodle! Important to the recipe is the inclusion of Agar Agar which helps give the broth a slight gel like consistency, mirroring the gelatin in authentic Ramen. This recipe can be improved by adding vegetables or various vegan proteins, but the key part of this simple recipe is that it is lazy ramen!

Ingredients

  • 1 serving of Ramen Noodles
  • Vegan Chicken Bullion
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 tsp MSG
  • 1 tsp Agar Agar Powder
  • Green Onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil. Stir in "Chicken" Bullion. After "Chicken" Bullion dissolved, add agar agar and MSG. Allow to cool some.
  2. Boil Ramen noodles in separate water, cook until al dente.
  3. In serving bowl, add cooked noodles then broth. Top with green onions.
